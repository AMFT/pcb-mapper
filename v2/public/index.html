              🐾 Getting Started

              1
              Load board photos using 📷 Top / 📷 Bottom
              2
              Place alignment points (A) on both sides, hit 📐 Align
              3
              Draw the board outline (O) — click corners, Enter to close
              4
              Add components (C) — drag outline, then click to place each pin
              5
              Place markers (M) for vias/through-holes
              6
              Draw traces (T) — angle snaps to 45°, snaps to pads
              7
              Add pours (P), use punchout (X) for clearances
              8
              Measure distances with 📏 Measure (U)
              9
              Export ⚡ Gerber or view in 🧊 3D
